My Recommendation

Use #137a30 as the brand anchor

It's the darkest green that still reads unmistakably as green, not "dark olive" or "forest generic." At 5.10:1 on cream, it passes WCAG AA for all text sizes. It's close to your current --green-dark (#0d7a36) but slightly warmer — less blue undertone, which harmonizes better with the warm cream background.

The vibrant --green (#22c55e) stays in the system for decorative use: filled buttons, dots, borders, icon fills. It should never be text.

Drop the current logo green (#16a34a) entirely — it's an orphan that serves no role the other two can't handle better.
