Palette Options for Plepic

4 palettes in the neighborhood of #22c55e. Same anchors: brand #137a30 + accent #d97757. Each varies the vivid green by a few degrees of hue or saturation. Structure: surface → light → vivid → brand → dark.

Theory behind the options
A (#22c55e) is the baseline at hue ~142°. The 3 alternatives explore small moves: B goes 5° warmer (toward yellow), C goes 10° cooler (toward teal), D stays at the same hue but maxes out saturation. All four are unmistakably "green" — the differences are subtle but visible in context.

What to watch for: How does the vivid green feel as a dot accent on cream? As a heading color on dark? As the butterfly fill? As a badge border? The brand green and orange accent stay identical across all four.
