Forewing Variations — 12 Options

Hindwings use #10 streamlined (primary) or #5 wide fan. Experimenting with different forewing shapes.

1. Wide Angular (Original)

Reference — current forewing shape

2. Tall Narrow

Wings point more upward, less wide

3. Swept Back

Sharp tips swept backwards like a jet

4. Rounded Top

Softer top edge, less angular tips

5. Compact

Smaller wingspan, tighter to body

6. Extra Wide

Maximum horizontal spread

7. Fewer Facets

Only 4 large panels per wing — cleaner

8. Flat Horizontal

Wings extend more sideways than upward

9. Single Point

One sharp angular tip per wing

10. Moth-like

Wider, squatter — moth proportions

11. V-Shape

Wings angled up forming a V

12. Double Point

Two angular tips per wing — closer to sketch
