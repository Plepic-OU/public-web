Design decisions

  • Single hue (137°) for all greens — no hue drift between shades.
  • System saturation: 73%. Surface, light, brand, and dark all share S=73%. One simple rule.
  • Vivid exception: S=100%. The only color at max saturation — used for decorative punch (dots, fills, dark-mode headings). Never as text on light backgrounds.
  • Accent at H=15°, S=73% — split-complementary of 137°, saturation matched to brand. Color theory relationship is inherent.
  • One accent color only. No dark/hover variants. Use opacity for states.
  • Green = structural identity. Logo, headings, navigation, badges, links, cards.
  • Orange = action warmth. CTA button and urgency indicators. One per viewport.
  • WCAG: Brand passes AA on cream (5.03:1) and white (5.38:1). Dark passes AAA on cream (8.07:1). Accent passes AA-large on light, AA on dark (5.25:1).
