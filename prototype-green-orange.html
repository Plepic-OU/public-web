8 — Hierarchy recommendation

Use orange sparingly. Green owns the brand.

Green = structural identity. Logo, headings, navigation, badges, links, cards. It's Plepic's color.

Orange = action warmth. The primary CTA button, urgency badges ("4 spots left"), and the Anthropic partnership signal. One orange element per viewport is the sweet spot.

The stress test (Section 7) shows the limit: when both colors appear at equal density, it does start feeling like a holiday decoration. The fix is simple — orange is the guest, green is the host.
