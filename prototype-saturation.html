Saturation Strategies for Plepic

Brand (S=73%) and accent (S=73%) are locked. 4 saturation distributions for the remaining shades: surface, light, vivid, dark. All at H=137°.

Research summary
Tailwind: Keeps saturation flat (61-84%) across their entire green scale — no dramatic curve.
Stripe: "Impossible colors" constrain saturation at extreme lightness — you can't have vivid near-white.
Key insight: At L=96%, even S=73% produces only a subtle tint. The real visual difference shows at L=87% (light) and L=39% (vivid). The vivid is where the saturation decision matters most.
